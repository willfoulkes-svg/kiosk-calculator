How to use this in a commercial conversation

  1. Get the missed-call rate. Most operators underestimate this. 5% is conservative - many phone-heavy restaurants lose 10-20% of calls during the Friday/Saturday dinner rush. A simple "how many calls does your POS show vs how many got answered?" almost always uncovers a bigger number.
  2. Pin down the call length honestly. 3-5 minutes is typical for a phone order with menu questions and payment. The labour line scales linearly with this number, so don't overstate it - a credible 4 minutes beats an aggressive 8.
  3. Enter the Phone AI monthly cost. The Net monthly value on the right shows what the restaurant actually pockets each month after subtracting the subscription and Flipdish's 5% commission on every order Phone AI handles.
  4. 5% commission is already baked in. You don't need to add it anywhere - the net figure already deducts 5% of (monthly phone orders × AOV). Mention this explicitly to the client so there are no surprises on invoice 1.
