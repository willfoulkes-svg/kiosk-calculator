How to use this

  1. Pick the country. Card processing costs differ between countries. Defaults reset when you switch.
  2. Step 1 — customer. Type in monthly Kiosk GMV, AOV, the processing fee and transaction fee the merchant is paying, and the monthly subscription. These drive Kiosk Software Net Revenue per month.
  3. Step 2 — hardware. Type the Kiosk hardware list price.
  4. Read the floor. The big number on the left is the lowest sales price the rep can charge. Don't sell below it.
  5. (Optional) Check a price. If the rep has a number in mind, type it into "Got a price in mind?" on the right. The payback period lights up green / amber / red — green is comfortable, amber is at the limit, red is over.
  6. Why 0.75 in the math? Finance applies a 25% cost-to-serve haircut, so the calculator approves discounts off real margin, not gross revenue. Click "Show me the math" if you want to see the full ladder.
  7. Kiosk-only, no CCEP. Coca-Cola's CCEP/CCH subsidy is tied to POS — a Kiosk-only deal doesn't qualify. For a deal with both POS and Kiosk, use the Combined calculator instead.
