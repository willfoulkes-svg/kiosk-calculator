How to use this at renewal

  1. Pick the country first. Card processing costs differ per country, which changes every SNR figure.
  2. Tick the products the customer actually uses. Untick anything they don't have so it drops out of the grand total. Online Ordering + POS are on by default; Kiosk is off.
  3. Fill in the Current column with whatever they're on today, then the New column with what you're proposing at renewal.
  4. Per-product cards show Current SNR, New SNR, and monthly + annual change for each line of business — so you can point to a single product if the conversation zooms in.
  5. Grand total at the top rolls everything up. The line-by-line table shows where each pound/euro/dollar comes from: sub, processing, transaction/end-user fees and card costs in one view.
  6. Tip: if you're keeping volumes the same across Current and New (common assumption in a renewal discussion), copy the Current GMV/AOV into New. Change only the pricing fields.
