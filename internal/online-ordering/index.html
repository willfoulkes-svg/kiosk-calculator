How to use this in commercial conversations

  1. Country first. Card processing costs vary a lot - UK 0.64% + £0.016, Ireland 0.48% + €0.018, USA 2.55% + $0.018, Spain 0.51% + €0.018. The wrong country gives the wrong SNR.
  2. Fill in the customer profile once. GMV, AOV, end-user fee, monthly sub, commission rate. Estimated orders/mo is calculated for you (GMV ÷ AOV).
  3. Calculator 1 (Total Fee): shows exactly what Flipdish nets each month from this deal. The breakdown shows where every pound/euro/dollar comes from.
  4. Calculator 2 (Sub vs Commission-Only): the Transfer-to-Subs argument. Type the customer's current commission-only rate in the box. Green hero = the sub model earns Flipdish more. The matrix shows the same comparison across every reasonable combination.
