How to use this

  1. Pick the deal type. POS only, Kiosk only, or POS + Kiosk combined. The inputs, countries and rules adjust to match.
  2. Pick the country. Card processing costs differ between countries. Kiosk-only deals are available in UK, IE, US and ES; POS and Combined deals are UK and IE only.
  3. Step 1 — customer. For POS or Kiosk, type monthly GMV, AOV, processing and transaction fees, and subscription. For Combined, type total on-premise GMV, Kiosk share, AOV, and per-channel rates. These drive Software Net Revenue per month.
  4. Hardware. Type the hardware list price. On POS and Combined deals, type any approved CCEP (UK) or CCH (IE) subsidy amount — otherwise leave it at 0. Kiosk-only deals don't qualify for CCEP/CCH.
  5. Read the floor. The big number on the left is the lowest sales price the rep can charge. Don't sell below it.
  6. (Optional) Check a price. If the rep has a number in mind, type it into "Got a price in mind?" on the right. The payback period lights up green / amber / red — green is comfortable, amber is at the limit, red is over.
  7. Why 0.75 in the math? Finance applies a 25% cost-to-serve haircut, so the calculator approves discounts off real margin, not gross revenue. Click "Show me the math" if you want to see the full ladder.
